About the App

IWNDWYToday is designed with simplicity and intention. This minimalist day counter app helps users track personal goals, particularly focused on sobriety, habit breaking, or habit formation.

No Clutter. No Guilt. Just Progress.

Focus on what matters: staying clear of bad habits today.

Privacy-Focused

All data is stored locally on your device. No sign-up required.

Supportive Approach

Encourages continuous improvement without shame. Restart your streak anytime while keeping your journey history.
